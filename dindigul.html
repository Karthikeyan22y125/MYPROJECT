DINDIGUL

Dindigul is famous for its biryani. The Dindigul biryani chains are famous all over Tamil Nadu.
Dindigul biryani is a common and popular dish, and Dindigul is sometimes called Biryani City.
Sherumalai Mountain, which is the home for a very special variety of small but sweet bananas, is located in the vicinity
