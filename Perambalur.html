Perambalur

The District has Vellar River in the North and it has well marked natural divisions.
The ground water resource through tube wells and wells are the most important source of irrigation.
The major crops grown in the district are paddy, groundnut, sugarcane, millets and Cashew.
Perambalur accounts for about 24% of the small onion produced in Tamil Nadu and holds the first place in its production in the State.
As per 2011 Census, the total Population of Perambalur District is 5,65,223. The density of population in the district is 321 per Sq.Km
